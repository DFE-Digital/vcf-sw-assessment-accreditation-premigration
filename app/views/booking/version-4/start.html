Book your social worker assessment

If you’re a social worker, use this service to:

  • book your practitioner social worker assessment
  • change or cancel your practitioner social worker assessment

Create an account to book social worker assessments

You must create an account to book a social worker assessment. You’ll get your username and password when you create your account.

Book your social worker assessment online

You’ll need your:

  • username
  • password
  • manager's email address (optional)
Start now

Change or cancel an assessment

You must give at least 3 full working days’ notice to change or cancel your assessment.
